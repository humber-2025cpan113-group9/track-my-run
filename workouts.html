Workouts

Long Run

Purpose of the workout is to keep a steady paca, its to build aerobic capacity and muscular endurance while training the body to efficiently use energy over extended distances.

Easy Run

Is a low-intensity, recovery-focused workout desgined to build aerobic fitness without excessive strain. Helps maintain millage while allowing the body to adapt and recover from harder sessions.

Tempo Run

is a controlled, moderately hard effort designed to improve your lactate threshold - the pace you can sustain for an extended period before fatigue build up.

Fartlek Run

its unstructered speed workout that alternates between fast and slow running.

Progression Run

is a sturucted workout that starts at an easy pace and gradually increases to a faster affort, teaching the body to run strong even when fatigued.

Interval traning

alternates between short, high-intensity efforts and recovery periods to boost speed, power and cardiovascular fitness. its highly effective for improving race performance, vo2 max and anaerobic capcity.

Hill Repeats

its short intense bursts of uphil running followed by recovery, desgined to build strength, power and running efficiency. They improve leg turnover, aerobic capcity and mental toughness while reduicing injusry risk compared to flat speedwork.

Recovery Run

is a short, easy-paced run desgined to promote active recovery between harder workouts. it enhances blood flow to muscles, aids in flushing out metabolic wasrte, and helps maintain running consistency without adding fatigue.
